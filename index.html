Welcome!

My name is Stephen Case and this webpage is my digital resume. This website allows you to get to know me as a graphic designer and my life story.

Below you will have a brief timeline of my academic and work experience.

On the navigation bar you will be able to view my personal journey as a graphic designer and my future plans as a graphic designer.

Below each page you will see a link to my Behance Portfolio so you can view my digital portfolio.



Mission Oak High School (2011-2015)

I graduated from Mission Oak High School as a co-valedictorian, a California Scholastic Federation Lifetime Member, and with high honors in English, Math, Social Studies, and Science.

I also volunteered with Harvesting Hope Tulare County and helped redesign the Varsity Football helmets during my junior year in high school and even made poster designs for football games!



College of the Sequoias (2015-2024)

I will be graduating from the College of the Sequoias with an Associate of Science in Graphic Design. I have learned how to use Adobe Creative Cloud software and have developed technical skills in logo design, brand identity, photography, and multimedia design.

My time at COS has been fruitful as it has allowed me to develop new perspectives and points of view in life while also helping polish and refine my technical skills in the field of graphic design.



Visalia Rawhide (2024-Pres)

On April 2024, I will begin a Graphic Design and Marketinginternship for the Visalia Rawhide Baseball Club that will be for their 2024 Regular Season. I will be creating graphic design creations and creating stadium photography compositions for promotional use.

I also will be learning how to do marketing and community outreach to bring in fans old and new to Rawhide games and to expand the team's brand all across Tulare County.
